Local Transfer

It involves sending money to a recipient's local bank account through a network of financial institutions. An intermediary organisation or financial institution facilitates the transfer by coomunicating between the payer and the recipient.

Benefit:

- It is more convenient to transfer money online as compared to going to a physical ATM machine to transfer the money to another party.

- Fast and efficient.

Overseas Transfer

The process of sending money or assets from one country to another. It involves transferring funds across borders to a recipient in a different country.

Benefit:

- Financial Support: Overseas transfers can provide financial support to individuals in need. For example, someone working abroad can send money back to their own home country to support their family or pay for their own expenses.

- Investment Opportunities: Overseas transfers can faciliate investments in foreign countries. Individuals can transfer funds to invest in real estate, stocks, or businesses abroad, potentially accessing new markets and divrsifying their portfolios.
